Marianne Williamson Just Went Viral When She Said This To Bring Down Trump

By
Anastacia Shelton
-
August 1, 2019
0
153
Share
Facebook
Twitter
ReddIt
Email
    - Advertisement -

    Marianne Williamson has not been taken seriously yet by many Americans.  I’ll admit, I didn’t take her seriously, during the first debate.

    A friend with whom I regularly discuss politics did, however.  He is a rather moderate Republican who insisted, prior to November 2016, that Trump would win.  I blew him off, as did most of us.  We were wrong.

    This friend took note of Williamson during that first debate.  And now he predicts that she could gain quite a following among progressives/liberals/the “left.”

    Judging by the internet fury last night – he’s right!  Williamson was the most Googled candidate during Tuesday night’s debate and she trended on the top on Twitter.  She went viral with her monologue about environmental injustice.

    And there’s memes abounding about her, too.   And what my 23 year old voting daughter calls “Twitter speak.” (or something like that!)  There’s probably even merch.

    She’s smart.  She’s different.  She’s charismatic.  I mean, Oprah loved her.  The Millennials love her. She got the most Google interest in the second debate.

    Most importantly, we have GOT to pick the person who can beat Donald Trump in 2020.  Do we GO with someone who couldn’t get there last time?  Or with Elizabeth Warren?  Or Joe?  Or DNC darling Kampala?

    Or do we try something different?  Remember, GET OUT AND VOTE!
